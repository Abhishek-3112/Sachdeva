About Us

Committed to accuracy, care, and building long-term relationships with our patients.

Our Story

Sachdeva Diagnostics started in 1994 as an Ultrasound Scan Centre which expanded into a complete Diagnostic Centre in 1998, with the start of the Pathology and Radiology services, doing all basic tests required for day to day treatment.

It was established, and has since operated, on the principles of providing the most reliable and accurate reports possible, using the state of the art technology under experienced hands. There is no compromise on the protocols for doing any test and the set rules are followed both by the doctors and the staff.

The machines are replaced every few years with ones having the latest technology and the doctors and technicians are trained regularly to upgrade their skills accordingly. In an age of marketing and promotions it is one of the few centres which runs without a marketing team, as Sachdeva Diagnostics relies on its work to speak for itself.

The Doctors

Dr. Sanjeev Sachdeva

MBBS, MD (Radiology)

Dr. Sanjeev Sachdeva is an alumnus from the historic Sarojini Naidu Medical College, Agra with training stints at Safdarjung Hospital and AIIMS, New Delhi. He has an experience of more than 30 years and worked as a Radiologist at the DNRI, Aashlok Hospital, Delhi Scan Centre Sir Gangaram Hospital and Moolchand Hospital before starting Sachdeva Diagnostics.

Dr. Savita Sachdeva

MBBS, M.D (Pathology)

Dr. Savita Sachdeva is an alumnus from the prestigious King George Medical College (KGMC) Lucknow. After post graduation she joined Moolchand Hospital and worked as a Pathologist for two years. She has more than twenty eight years of experience in this field. Her areas of excellence are haematology, clinical biochemistry and cytology. Her continuous presence in the clinic and supervision in each test conducted, is the reason behind the quality and reliability of reports at Sachdeva Diagnostics.

The Staff

Staff at Sachdeva Diagnostics is polite, experienced and ready to help patients at all times. Lab-technicians and Radiographers are properly trained and experienced. Phlebotomists are experienced and well trained to draw samples from patients of all ages, especially new born, infants and elderly.
