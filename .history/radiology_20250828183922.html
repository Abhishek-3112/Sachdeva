Radiography uses electromagnetic radiation to view bones and tissues. It is fast, painless, and non-invasive.

Key Equipment at Sachdeva Diagnostics:

  • High Frequency 600mA X-ray Machine (Medtronics)
  • AGFA CR System for Digital X-rays

Safety Note:

Radiation is very low with digital systems. Avoided in pregnancy unless necessary (baby shielded with lead).

Common X-rays:

  • Chest → lungs, heart, chest wall
  • Abdomen → obstruction, stones
  • Dental → cavities, bone loss
  • Bone → fractures, arthritis

Special Procedures:

  • Orthopantomogram (OPG) → full jaw scan
  • HSG (Hysterosalpingography) → uterus & tubes (infertility workup)
  • IVP (Intravenous Pyelography) → kidneys, ureters, bladder
  • Barium Studies → GI tract (swallow, meal, enema)

Ultrasound uses high-frequency sound waves for imaging. It is safe, painless, and radiation-free.

Equipment:

RS 85 Prestige Whole Body Ultrasound (Samsung) with Convex, Linear, TVS/TRUS & 3D/4D probes.

Common Uses:

  • Obstetric → monitor pregnancy, fetal growth
  • Pelvic → uterus, ovaries, bladder
  • Abdomen → liver, kidney, pancreas
  • Vascular (Color Doppler) → blood flow in veins & arteries
  • Musculoskeletal → joints, tendons

Specialized Techniques:

  • 3D/4D Ultrasound → fetus/organ in 3D view
  • Color Doppler → blood flow mapping
  • Shearwave Elastography → liver fibrosis, tumor stiffness
  • Fat Quantification (QUS) → liver fat %
