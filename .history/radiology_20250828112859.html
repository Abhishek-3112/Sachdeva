A. Radiology Services Overview

Our Radiology & Imaging Department provides a wide range of diagnostic imaging services using the latest technology. These services help in early disease detection, accurate diagnosis, and effective treatment planning.

B. Imaging Modalities

We offer multiple advanced imaging modalities including:

  • Digital X-Ray – High-resolution imaging for bones and chest diagnosis.
  • Ultrasound – Safe and painless imaging for abdominal, obstetric, and vascular assessments.
  • CT Scan – Detailed cross-sectional imaging for internal organs and tissues.
  • MRI – Advanced imaging for brain, spine, joints, and soft tissues.
  • Mammography – Breast cancer screening with digital accuracy.
